Digital Strategy

Marketing strategy identifies your competitive advantages. It determines the most effective promotion channels, calculates budgets for comprehensive marketing for the next two years, shows how to develop your company, brand and product.
Сontact us
Positioning
We gather data and create a concept of perception of your product, which we will convey to the audience.
Designing
We design a marketing system and show how to promote in different ways for the next two years.
We set KPI, calculate the budgets and determine the ROMI. The minimum allowable ROMI for us is 100%.
The strategy determines the effectiveness of the next steps.
  • It is important to correctly identify the advantages and channels for integrated marketing. This way the brand will quickly become relevant and recognizable, and the promotion will be cost-effective and bring sales.
  • Measurable promotion We maximize the number of requests within a given budget. We bring applications in the first month.
  • We focus on sales. We constantly adjust the rates in the advertising offices.
  • We use services to automate advertising. We make content which makes people want to buy.
Synergy We increase the effectiveness of promotion due to synergy with the content marketing.
Measurability We use call tracking, web analytics and dynamic reports to accurately determine the profitability of promotion.
Brand impact We use your brand identity to increase the effectiveness of your promotion as well as expand your brand's audience.
