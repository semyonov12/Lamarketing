The sense of smell is one of the most vulnerable human senses, because smells affect the imagination and emotions. Smells trigger our memory. A child's sense of smell comes into play first, ahead of sight and taste. What is really important is that smell influences decision-making. ScentAir company approached us to promote its activities in one other country.

Solution We have developed a new website for the company that specializes in the aromatization of commercial premises. The task of the website was to introduce the user to the company's products and create an image and brand credibility in the market of a new country.

We analyzed the situation on the fragrance market and positioned the company as one of the best companies for premise aromatization, thanks to which such giants as Zara Home, Ikea and others have chosen ScentAir as a partner.

We also created the structure of the site for promotion, the distribution of queries to relevant pages and internal relinking are the parameters that should be taken into account at the stage of development of a new site.

Result We worked out every detail, so we were able to convey brand values, create emotions and push to purchase. The site is adapted for viewing from a computer, tablet and smartphone.
