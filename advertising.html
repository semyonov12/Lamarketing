Targeted advertising

We make social networks a full-fledged sales tool. We work with Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter. We find any target audiences, including those who are not available directly through the interface of the advertising cabinet. We extensively use automation: splitting campaigns into narrow segments, unloading products for online stores, and much more.
Сontact us
We devise campaigns from the general to the particular, dividing the total target audience into smaller segments based on geography, age, profession, devices, activity in thematic publishers, and travels to other countries. We tailor the message for each segment and the graphic designers develop the creatives.
